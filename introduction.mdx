## Overview

LendSquawk empowers loan officers with structured intelligence for lender overlays and decision support before loan submission. You get real-time insights into lender eligibility, pricing scenarios, and down payment assistance programs tailored to borrower profiles like FICO scores, LTV ratios, and DTI limits.

Key benefits include faster lender matching, reduced submission guesswork, and optimized borrower structures. Access the platform at [app.lendsquawk.com](https://app.lendsquawk.com) to start closing smarter.
