---
title: Cannot View Loans Dashboard
description: Fix for missing or blank loans dashboard in LendSquawk.
---

## Symptoms

- Loans dashboard is blank
- "No loans found" error shown

## Causes

- Not logged in
- Filters set incorrectly

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check login status">
    Log out and log in again to `https://app.lendsquawk.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset filters">
    Click **Reset Filters** above the loans table.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Try refreshing or clearing your browser cache.
  </Step>
</Steps>
