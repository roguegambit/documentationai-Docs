## Resetting Password

Forgot your password? Follow these steps:

1. Go to `https://app.lendsquawk.com/login`
2. Click **Forgot Password?**
3. Enter your registered email address.
4. Click **Send Reset Link**.
5. Check your inbox for a reset email.
6. Follow the instructions to set a new password.

<Callout kind="info">
Reset link expires in 30 minutes.
</Callout>

If you don’t see the email, check your spam folder or contact support.
