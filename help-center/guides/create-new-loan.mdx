## Quick Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Loans">
    Log in and click **Loans** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start New Listing">
    Click **New Loan**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Details">
    Enter loan amount, purpose, and terms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Submit">
    Double check details, then click **Submit**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
You’ll receive a confirmation email after your loan is listed.
</Callout>
