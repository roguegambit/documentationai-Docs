## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
Ensure you have:
- A valid email address for account verification
- Basic borrower details ready (e.g., FICO score, LTV, DTI)
- Access to `app.lendsquawk.com`
</Callout>

LendSquawk helps loan officers find the best lender matches based on overlays and eligibility before submission.

## Create and Verify Your Account

Follow these steps to get started in under 2 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit [`app.lendsquawk.com`](https://app.lendsquawk.com) and click **Sign Up**.

    Enter your email, create a password, and select your role (Loan Officer or Broker).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for the verification email from LendSquawk.

    Click the verification link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the login page and sign in with your verified credentials.

    You'll land on the dashboard ready for your first scenario.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Navigate the Dashboard

The dashboard provides quick access to key tools like Lender Match, Scenario Comparison, and DPA Intelligence.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Main Sections" icon="layout">
    - **Lender Match**: Compare lenders by structure (e.g., FICO 660, LTV 75%).
    - **Scenario Comparison**: Switch between Conv 30yr Fixed, FHA, VA IRRRL.
    - **DPA Programs**: Surface assistance like CA HFA MyHome ($15,000).
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Active Scenario View" icon="target">
    View details like:
    
    | Lender | Rate | LTV | Score |
    |--------|------|-----|-------|
    | Pacific Home Loans | 6.875% | 85% | 100 |
    | Change Wholesale | 7.125% | 80% | 95 |
    
    Use the side-by-side compare for top lenders.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Run Your First Lender Match Scenario

Test with a sample purchase scenario: $262,000 loan, FICO 660, LTV 75%, DTI 42%.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Borrower Profile" icon="user">
    Click **New Scenario** > **Purchase**.

    Input:
    - FICO: `660`
    - LTV: `75%`
    - DTI: `42%`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Product" icon="package">
    Choose **Conv 30yr Fixed** as your active scenario.

    Toggle to compare with **FHA 30yr Fixed** or **VA IRRRL**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run Lender Match" icon="search">
    Click **Run Match**. Review results:

    Top match: Pacific Home Loans (6.875%, 85% LTV, score 100).

    Generate the Pricing Memo for submission.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Share" icon="save">
    Save the scenario and export the memo PDF.

    Share via email or integrate with your LOS.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip" title="Pro Tip">
Start with conservative inputs (e.g., FICO `<660`, DTI `>45%`) to see AUS Rescue suggestions like adding reserves or co-borrower income.
</Callout>
